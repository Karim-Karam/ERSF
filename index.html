About us

The federation is a pioneering platform aimed at empowering youth in advanced technological fields such as Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, RC Sports, Unmanned Vehicle, and Programming. By organizing training events and technological and sports competitions, we strive to enhance youth skills and motivate them to excel and compete on a global level. We also focus on supporting digital transformation and strengthening Egypt's position as a regional hub for innovation, contributing to the realization of Egypt's Vision 2030 and driving sustainable development.

Mission

Under the supervision and patronage of the Ministry of Youth and Sports, and in collaboration with national and international institutions, the federation seeks to lead in developing a creative and innovative digital generation equipped with advanced skills and knowledge in fields such as robotics, artificial intelligence, RC sports, unmanned vehicles, and programming.

Rules & Regulations

• Adherence to the Federation's articles of association, internal regulations, and the decisions of the General Assembly and Board of Directors, and compliance with their implementation.

• Payment of annual fees within the specified time, according to the rules and conditions in the regulations.

• Striving to achieve the Federation's objectives and refraining from actions or practices that may negatively impact its identity or damage its reputation.

• Respect for principles, values, traditions, and the spirit of fair competition.

• Compliance with all duties arising from applying the Federation's terms and conditions.

• Observance of public decency while in the Federation.

• Proactively updating membership information when any changes occur.

• Active participation in Federation's activities, events, and programs.

• Compliance with the licensed venues for Federation activities, subject to the terms and conditions issued by the Federation, with violators held accountable and penalties applied as per the regulations.

• Providing opportunities for all individuals to participate in the Federation's championships and competitions, according to the approved regulations and rules. Specific procedures must be followed when participating in external championships without Federation approval, with the individual assuming the consequences.

• Responsibility for maintaining and operating the equipment used, and for any physical or material damage resulting, whether during official competitions, activities, events, festivals, or other events inside or outside Egypt.

• Responsibility for any violation of membership terms or misuse.
